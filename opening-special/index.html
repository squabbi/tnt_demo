Talents and Treasures Financial Group is a business which seeks to be a ministry to:

  1. help people become better stewards of their finances and
  2. support the spread of the Gospel

The Group does it in multiple ways. At the basic level, the Group will handle all individual and business tax returns, and will direct a portion of all fees to the work of Gospel ministry. For this financial year, T&T Financial Group is committed to giving to theological education - specifically, providing scholarships for students in financial need. We are aiming to raise up to $20,000 through our generous clients and partners.

The Group is offering an introductory rate for the first 50 clients. Individual tax returns can range between $225-$345 (depending on the complexity). But as an introductory price, we will do individual tax returns for a fixed rate of $175 per new client. Of this amount, $100 will go directly to ministry and the remainder will be split between GST and basic business costs. We will provide a tax invoice for the service upon completion. The $175 can be claimed as a tax deduction for the next financial year. Our hope is that this will help our clients manage their finances better while also supporting vital ministries that blesses the wider church.

We also provide a full suite of financial services ranging from superannuation, investments, debt management and bookkeeping services. Our commitment for all of these services is the same: excellent service for our clients and appropriate support to ministries. The amount we give to ministries based on these other services will vary as the fee is different dependent upon client needs. However, you can be confident that what you pay will not only meet your financial needs; it will also support the areas we have agreed to fund.

We will provide annual reports for the ministries we support, and we welcome recommendations from our clients.
